# Accumulation Patterns

## Classic Accumulation (Wyckoff Model)

1. **PS** → SC → AR → ST forms the preliminary structure
2. **Phase B** builds the cause (6-8 months typical)
3. **Spring** tests the floor with low volume
4. **SOS** confirms demand
5. **LPS** establishes support at breakout level
6. **Phase E** marks the markup

## Visual Indicators

| Pattern | Price | Volume | Phase |
|---------|-------|--------|-------|
| Climax | High spike | Very high | A |
| Quiet absorption | Narrow range | Variable | B |
| Shakeout | Below TR | Low then high | C |
| Breakout | Above TR | Expanding | D |
| Rally | Strong up | High | E |

## Distribution Patterns (Opposite)

- Climax at top → AR → ST → UTAD → SOS/SOW breakdown
- Spring = Upthrust After Distribution (UTAD)
