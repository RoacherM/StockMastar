# Wyckoff Events Reference

## Key Events Identification

### Buying Climax (BC) / Selling Climax (SC)

- **What**: Extreme high-volume spike at trend end
- **Identification**: Wide range bar with volume > 2x average
- **Interpretation**: Smart money distributing/absorbing final positions

### Automatic Rally (AR) / Automatic Reaction (AR)

- **What**: Immediate countermove after climax
- **Identification**: Opposite direction move next 2-5 bars
- **Interpretation**: Initial supply/demand imbalance correction

### Secondary Test (ST)

- **What**: Retest of climax price level
- **Identification**: Price returns to BC/SC level on lower volume
- **Interpretation**: Weak hands testing; strong hands absorbing

### Spring (Accumulation) / UTAD (Distribution)

- **What**: Final test below/above trading range
- **Identification**: Breach of TR boundary, rapid reversal
- **Interpretation**: Smart money shaking out remaining weak holders

### Sign of Strength (SOS) / Sign of Weakness (SOW)

- **What**: Confirms new trend direction
- **Identification**: Breakout from TR with expansion
- **Interpretation**: Demand overcoming supply (SOS) or vice versa

### Last Point of Support (LPS) / Last Point of Supply (LPSY)

- **What**: Final support/resistance before trend acceleration
- **Identification**: Pullback to breakout level with contraction
- **Interpretation**: Final accumulation point before markup

## Price-Volume Rules

| Price Action | Volume | Interpretation |
|--------------|--------|----------------|
| Up | High | Demand absorbing supply - Bullish |
| Up | Low | No demand - Weakness |
| Down | High | Supply overwhelming demand - Bearish |
| Down | Low | No supply - Strength |
